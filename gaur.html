Gaur

The gaur (Bos gaurus), also known as the Indian bison, is an Asian bovine. It is the only living species in the genus Bos and the third-largest extant species of bovid, after African buffalo and Cape buffalo. The gaur is native to the Indian subcontinent and Southeast Asia, and is listed as Vulnerable on the IUCN Red List because of habitat loss and fragmentation. It is the national animal of India and Sri Lanka.
